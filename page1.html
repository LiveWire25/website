Aenigma Syntax 

Aenigma Syntax is an interactive online cryptic hunt where
players are given questions, hints, and clues. To solve questions, participants
must scour the web, decode ciphers, and interpret pop culture references to
uncover answers.

Aperture Alchemy

Explore the striking contrast between light and shadow in our "Light & Dark" theme. Through techniques like backlighting and reflections, this event delves into emotions, mystery, and storytelling, capturing the balance between clarity and the unknown. Experience how light shapes perception and evokes deeper meanings. 

APPetite

Create an innovative health app that tracks meal calories and suggests recipes based on dietary goals. Develop an original app, submit an APK, source code, and a short video, and compete for the top spot! Finalists will present their app, explaining its design, functionality, and innovation.
Judging Criteria: Functionality, UI Design, Code Quality, Innovation & Creativity.



Frame By Frame

Frame by Frame challenges students (Classes IX-XII) to create a 5-7 minute short film on the theme "The Multiverse of Misunderstandings," showcasing how small misunderstandings can have a big impact with a humorous twist. The preliminary round is online, where participants submit their films as YouTube Unlisted links by April 25. The top 5 teams will advance to the offline final round, where they will present their films to judges, followed by a Q&A session. Films will be judged on creativity, theme relevance, content quality, cinematography, and humor.










HackerRank

HackerRank is a platform designed to help developers of all levels sharpen their coding skills through real-world challenges and competitions. Offering a wide range of problems across algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and more, HackerRank provides an interactive environment to practice, learn, and grow. Whether you're a beginner looking to improve or an expert aiming to solve complex problems, HackerRank is the ultimate tool for coding practice and career advancement.



Venture Verse

Venture Verse 2025 is an exhilarating entrepreneurship competition for students in grades IX to XII, challenging them to develop innovative AI-powered solutions for small businesses. Teams will first submit an online prototype and pitch video addressing real-world problems like automation, marketing, or customer support. The top five teams advance to the final round, where they'll craft a live business pitch and website for a surprise industry—all within 100 minutes! With a focus on creativity, AI integration, and real-world impact, this event is the perfect platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to shine. Join the competition and bring your boldest ideas to life!



Voxel Visionaries

Voxel Visionaries is an innovative 3D designing competition that invites creative minds to push the boundaries of digital artistry and engineering. this contest challenges participants to craft stunning, functional, or imaginative designs using voxel-based modeling techniques. Voxels, the 3D equivalent of pixels, offer a unique sandbox for creators to build intricate structures, blending precision with artistic freedom.
Participants are judged on creativity, technical skill, and originality. The competition not only celebrates 3D design but also fosters a community of forward-thinking visionaries eager to shape the future of digital creation.
Whether you’re a seasoned designer or a curious newcomer, Voxel Visionaries is your chance to showcase your vision and leave a mark in the 3D world!


Dynamic Racing Showdown

Dynamic Racing Showdown (DRS)  is an electrifying engineering challenge where teams of four design, build, and race custom wireless cars (max 8kg, 30×20×20cm) through three intense rounds. Competitors must balance raw power and precise control while executing strategic overtakes - all while avoiding track penalties. Held on May 3rd at school premises, this safety-focused event tests innovation, driving skill, and teamwork under pressure. Cars are judged on four criteria: acceleration (Power), handling (Control), overtaking tactics (Aggression), and fastest clean laps (Race Performance). Ready to engineer victory?


Ascension

Live Wire Gaming 2025 is the ultimate esports showdown for classes IX-XII, featuring VALORANT (5v5), BGMI (2v2), and Brawlhalla (2v2). Compete in VALORANT’s Swiftplay brackets, BGMI’s 3-match Erangel survival, and Brawlhalla’s crew battles (online prelims → offline finals). Strict fair-play rules apply, with points awarded for kills, placements, and skill. Bring your A-game and peripherals for finals!
